How to Export Your WhatsApp Chats

For iPhone (iOS)

  1. 1 Open the WhatsApp chat you want to export.
  2. 2 Tap on the contact or group name at the top of the screen.
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap on 'Export Chat'.
  4. 4 A pop-up will appear. Choose the 'Without Media' option. This creates a .txt file, which is what our tool needs.
  5. 5 Save the exported .txt file to your device or a cloud service (like iCloud Drive or Google Drive) so you can access it from your computer.

For Android

  1. 1 Open the WhatsApp chat you wish to export.
  2. 2 Tap the three-dot menu icon (â‹®) in the top-right corner.
  3. 3 Tap on 'More', and then select 'Export chat'.
  4. 4 You will be prompted to include media. Select 'WITHOUT MEDIA' to ensure you get the required .txt file.
  5. 5 Choose how you want to save or send the file (e.g., email it to yourself, save to Google Drive) to easily access it on your computer.
