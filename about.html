Our Mission

We believe memories are precious. In a world of fleeting digital conversations, our mission is to provide a beautiful and private way to preserve the chats that define our most important relationships.

The Story Behind WhatsApp Replay

WhatsApp Replay started from a simple, personal need: wanting to reread old conversations with loved ones without endlessly scrolling or squinting at a plain text file. We realized that the joy of these memories wasn't just in the words, but in the familiar back-and-forth flow of the chat interface itself.

We set out to build a tool that was not only functional but also delightful to use. Most importantly, we built it with privacy at its core. Your memories are yours alone, which is why our tool processes everything directly in your browser. Your chat files are never uploaded to a server, ensuring your conversations remain completely private.
